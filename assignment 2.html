The Solar System

Our solar system consists of an average star we call the Sun, the planets

  • Mercury
  • Venus
  • Earth
  • Mars
  • Jupiter
  • Saturn
  • Uranus
  • Neptune and
  • Pluto
It includes: the satellites of the planets; numerous comets, asteroids, and meteoroids; and the interplanetary medium.
